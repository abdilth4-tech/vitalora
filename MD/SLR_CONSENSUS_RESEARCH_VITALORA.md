**Key Themes Across All 5 Diseases:**
1. **Novel Biomarkers:** Exosomal proteins, microRNAs, metabolomic signatures, adipokines
2. **Imaging Integration:** Ultrasound, transient elastography, MRI for noninvasive diagnosis
3. **Pharmacogenomics:** Genetic variants influencing therapeutic response (e.g., APOB rs1042034)
4. **Machine Learning:** Integration of clinical, biochemical, microbiome data for risk stratification
5. **Herbal & Dietary Interventions:** Metabolomics-confirmed mechanisms of traditional treatments
6. **Equity & Access:** Disparities in screening uptake and treatment goal attainment across populations
7. **Comorbidity Mechanisms:** Shared pathways (insulin resistance, chronic inflammation, oxidative stress)

## VITALORA INTEGRATION RECOMMENDATIONS

**For Disease Risk Calculators:**
- Implement TyG-BMI for NAFLD/MetS screening
- Use TG/HDL-C ratios (sex-stratified) for T2DM early detection
- Integrate osteogenic biomarkers + LV-STE for T2DM family risk stratification

**For Patient Education:**
- Pathophysiology: Emphasize ceramides, pro-inflammatory cytokines, hepatic steatosis in metabolic disease cascade
- Evidence-based interventions: GLP-1RA for weight loss + glycemic control; metformin for prediabetes; lifestyle modifications

**For Clinical Decision Support:**
- Dyslipidemia: Non-HDL-C targeting; combination LLT for residual TG-rich lipoprotein risk
- HTN: Identify secondary causes (PA in resistant HTN); lifestyle + pharmacotherapy optimization
- NAFLD: Early noninvasive fibrosis assessment using transient elastography + FIB-4; screen for cirrhosis

**For Future Development:**
- Develop machine learning model combining multiple biomarkers (lipids, inflammatory markers, metabolomic signatures)
- Integrate wearable data (BP, activity, heart rate) with lab biomarkers for continuous risk monitoring
- Validate herbal intervention efficacy through metabolomics-guided clinical trials
