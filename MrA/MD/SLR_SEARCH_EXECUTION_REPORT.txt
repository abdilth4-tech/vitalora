3. KEY FINDINGS - HIGH YIELD BIOMARKERS
================================================================================

DYSLIPIDEMIA:
✓ Atherogenic Lipid Indices (AIP, AC, RC) - diagnostic utility in metabolic disease
✓ Non-HDL-C - superior to LDL-C alone for residual CVD risk
✓ TG/HDL-C ratio - simple, accessible biomarker for metabolic dysfunction
✓ Metrnl (meteorin-like protein) - novel adipokine protective against postprandial hypertriglyceridemia
✓ APOB rs1042034 - pharmacogenomic variant influencing statin response (Vietnamese cohort)

TYPE 2 DIABETES:
✓ TyG Index (triglyceride-glucose) - AUC 0.72 for DKD prediction
✓ TyG-BMI - improved predictive value in obese populations
✓ Osteogenic biomarkers (osteocalcin, osteoprotegerin) - early myocardial dysfunction detection
✓ Albumin-corrected fructosamine - independent predictor of diabetic retinopathy
✓ Serum uric acid - OR 1.85 for DKD progression in meta-analysis of 8 RCTs
✓ MicroRNAs (miR-29a, miR-222, miR-132) - potential GDM screening markers
✓ Methylglyoxal - AGE pathway involvement in cerebral small vessel disease

NAFLD/MASLD:
✓ TyG-BMI composite score - AUC 0.819 for NAFLD diagnosis
✓ Six-variable screening index (age, BMI, HDL-C, HbA1c, ALT, albumin) - outperforms FLI/HSI
✓ LCAT (lecithin cholesterol acyltransferase) - exosomal biomarker for diabetes
✓ Serum metabolomic signatures - 942 differential metabolites identified
  - Core markers: maresin 1, canavaninosuccinate, paraxanthine, 1-methyluric acid
✓ MicroRNAs (miR-122, miR-155, miR-34a) - therapeutic targets + biomarkers
✓ Phosphatidylcholine dysregulation - age-related metabolic feature
✓ C-reactive protein - independent NAFLD risk factor (OR 2.89)
✓ Subgingival plaque metabolites - MASLD-periodontitis comorbidity mechanism

METABOLIC SYNDROME:
✓ Glucose metabolism markers (FPG, HbA1c, OGTT, HOMA-IR)
✓ Life's Simple 7 (LS7) score - inversely associated with MASLD (RR 0.80)
✓ Female-specific cutoffs for TG/HDL-C (2.73 vs. 3.72 in men)

HYPERTENSION:
✓ Primary aldosteronism biomarkers (aldosterone-to-renin ratio)
✓ Cardiometabolic burden assessment in resistant HTN
