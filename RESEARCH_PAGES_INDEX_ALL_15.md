## 🔬 EVIDENCE HIERARCHY

- **Grade A (High)**: Based on meta-analyses of multiple large RCTs or landmark prospective trials (N>5000).
- **Grade B (Moderate)**: Based on systematic reviews of cohort studies or large cross-sectional validations (N>500).
- **Grade C (Limited)**: Based on emerging clinical consensus, small trials, or early machine learning models (N<500).
