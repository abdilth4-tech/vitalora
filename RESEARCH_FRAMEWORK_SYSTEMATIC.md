## 🔍 HOW TO FIND OPEN ACCESS PAPERS

### Method 1: Direct Check
- Go to DOI link → Check for "Open Access" badge
- Look for CC-BY, CC-BY-NC, or similar license

### Method 2: PubMed Central
- Search: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/
- Filter by "Free full text"

### Method 3: Journal Websites
- Major open access journals:
  - PLOS ONE (100% open access)
  - BMJ (selective open access)
  - Frontiers (mostly open access)
  - Diabetology & Metabolic Syndrome (open access)
  - JMIR Health (open access)

### Method 4: Author Repositories
- ResearchGate
- Academia.edu
- Author institution repositories
