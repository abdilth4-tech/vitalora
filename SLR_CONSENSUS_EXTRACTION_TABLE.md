## 🔬 Quality Assessment (GRADE)

### Grade A (High Quality) — 5 Papers
1. **2025 Multi-Metabolite Dyslipidemia** — Prospective validation design with discovery + replication cohorts
2. **2025 AI-ECG Hypertension** — Massive training set (752K ECGs), external validation, outcome follow-up
3. **2021 Biomarker Profiling HTN** — Largest cohort (36,985), machine learning + longitudinal design
4. **2023 Plasma Metabolic Fingerprints MetS** — Largest MetS cohort (13,554), machine learning validation
5. **2020 CT-based MetS Screening** — Large sample (7,785), fully automated analysis, outcome correlation

### Grade B (Moderate Quality) — 4 Papers
6. **2023 HALP Score Dyslipidemia** — Large retrospective (7,192), but cross-sectional design
7. **2023 PMR Dyslipidemia** — Large population (14,269), but cross-sectional only
8. **2026 AIP-HTN in Youth** — Machine learning approach, but single-center China cohort
9. **2021 Lasso Biomarkers MetS** — Occupational population specific, may not generalize
