Safety & Contraindications

Key Safety Documentation

General: All 7 remedies show excellent safety in RCTs.

Curcumin: Avoid in gallstones (bile stimulant).

Cinnamon: Use Ceylon variety to avoid coumarin toxicity.

Pregnancy: Specific formulas contraindicated (Ginger, Cinnamon).
