Herbal Protocol

Project Overview

Project: Herbal & Traditional Medicine Review untuk Metabolic Disease

Tujuan: Systematic evaluation herbal remedies dengan GRADE quality assessment

Tipe: Narrative systematic review, PRISMA 2020 Compliant

Research Questions

Q1. Efficacy

Herbal mana yang punya evidence untuk dyslipidemia, hypertension, NAFLD, MetS, T2DM?

Q2. Compounds

Phytochemical compounds mana (curcumin, EGCG, allicin) yang terbukti klinis?

Q3. Dosage

Optimal dosages & formulations berdasarkan RCT evidence?

Q4-8. Safety & Gaps

Quality of evidence, safety, mechanisms, dan research gaps?

Study Selection

Stage 1: Title & Abstract

Automated + manual screening untuk herbal intervention keywords

Stage 2: Full-Text Review

Retrieve full texts, assess inclusion criteria, extract GRADE quality

Quality Control

90% inter-rater agreement target, dual review 10% abstracts

Data Extraction

Core Elements (25+)

  • Study characteristics: Author, year, DOI, design, n, duration
  • Intervention: Herbal name, extract type, active compounds, dosage
  • Outcomes: Biomarker changes (LDL, HDL, BP, FPG), effect sizes
  • Safety: Adverse events, drug-herb interactions, contraindications
  • Evidence: GRADE rating (A-D), risk of bias assessment

GRADE Categories

  • Grade A (High): Multiple RCTs, consistent, large n
  • Grade B (Moderate): RCTs dengan limitations atau multiple smaller trials
  • Grade C (Low): Few RCTs, small n, inconsistent (common untuk herbal)
  • Grade D (Very Low): Case series, observational only, high bias
