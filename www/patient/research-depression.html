🧪 Spesifikasi Tool Screening (VITALORA)
PHQ-9 Questionnaire (9 items)
  • 1. Little interest/pleasure in activities
  • 2. Feeling down/depressed
  • 3. Sleep problems
  • 4. Fatigue/low energy
  • 5. Appetite changes
  • 6. Self-criticism/failure feeling
  • 7. Concentration difficulty
  • 8. Slower/faster movements
  • 9. Suicidal ideation

✅ Rekomendasi Manajemen (Evidence-Based)
  • Screening universal: Utama dalam primary care, metabolic clinics, chronic disease management
  • Diagnosis: PHQ-9 score ≥ 10 indicates probable major depression
  • Assessment komorbid: Screen untuk metabolic syndrome, DM, HTN (bidirectional associations)
  • Intervention: Psychotherapy + lifestyle (exercise 30min/day, dietary counseling)
  • Pharmacotherapy: SSRIs indicated untuk moderate-severe depression; remission <25% dengan lifestyle alone
