✅ Rekomendasi Manajemen (Evidence-Based)
  • Screening: Males ≥40 years dengan sexual dysfunction, fatigue, mood changes
  • Diagnosis: Total testosterone < 300 ng/dL (morning fasting) + symptoms
  • Confirm: Repeat testing; evaluate for secondary causes (obesity, DM, medications)
  • Treatment: Lifestyle first (weight loss, exercise); TRT if symptomatic + confirmed low T
  • Monitor: PSA, hematocrit, symptom response at 3-6 months and annually
