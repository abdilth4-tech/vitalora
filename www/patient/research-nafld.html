✅ Rekomendasi Manajemen
  • Screening: Prioritas untuk pasien dengan metabolic syndrome, obesity, atau diabetes
  • Initial Assessment: Ultrasound screening + FIB-4 index calculation
  • Risk Stratification: Normal | Possible NASH | Advanced Fibrosis (berdasarkan FIB-4 cutoff)
  • Lifestyle Intervention: Weight loss 5-10%, exercise, diet modification
  • Referral to Hepatologist: If FIB-4 suggestive of advanced fibrosis atau ultrasound abnormal
  • Monitoring: Annual ultrasound + labs untuk high-risk patients
