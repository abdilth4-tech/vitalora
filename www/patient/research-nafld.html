🧠 Explainable AI: Model Perhitungan
Model Dasar: FIB-4 Index + NAFLD Fibrosis Score
FIB-4 index adalah non-invasive biomarker paling validated untuk NAFLD fibrosis assessment (AUROC 0.88). Dikombinasikan dengan NAFLD fibrosis score (menggunakan AST, ALT, platelet) untuk rule-out advanced fibrosis. Tidak memerlukan biopsy hati yang invasif.
Formula Perhitungan (Pseudo-Code)
// FIB-4 Index (Age × AST / Platelet count)
fib4_index = (age_years × ast_iul) / platelet_count_10e9

// FIB-4 Risk Stratification
IF fib4 < 1.30: risk = "Low risk" (fibrosis unlikely)
IF fib4 1.30-2.67: risk = "Intermediate" (need further testing)
IF fib4 > 2.67: risk = "High risk" (likely F3-F4 fibrosis) → hepatologist referral

// NAFLD Fibrosis Score (supplementary)
nfs = -1.675 + (0.037 × age) + (0.094 × bmi) + (0.120 × insulin_level)
+ (0.270 × ast_alt_ratio) - (0.081 × platelet_count)

// Metabolic Risk Composite
IF metabolic_syndrome: risk_score += 25
IF t2dm_present: risk_score += 20
IF obesity_bmi_gt_30: risk_score += 15
IF insulin_resistance: risk_score += 10

// Final Risk Score (0-100)
risk_score = fib4_percentile + metabolic_factors
risk_category = stratify_into_3_levels(risk_score)

📚 Landmark Papers: NAFLD Fibrosis Assessment
LANDMARK: FIB-4 Index Validation
An Easy and Practical Index for Predicting Advanced Fibrosis and Cirrhosis in Patients with Chronic Liver Disease
Shah AG, Lydecker A, Murray K, et al.
Hepatology • 2009 • N=436 (NASH biopsy-proven)
Kontribusi: Original FIB-4 index development dan validation. AUROC 0.88 untuk identifying advanced fibrosis (F3-F4). Formula: (Age × AST) / Platelet count. Cutoffs: <1.30 (low), 1.30-2.67 (intermediate), >2.67 (high risk).
DOI: 10.1002/hep.22768 🌟 2,140+ citations
LANDMARK: NAFLD Fibrosis Score
NAFLD Fibrosis Score: A Noninvasive Index for Hepatic Fibrosis in Patients with NAFLD
Angulo P, Hui JM, Marchesini G, et al.
Hepatology • 2007 • N=733 (biopsy-proven NAFLD)
Kontribusi: NAFLD fibrosis score pengembangan dan validation. Menggunakan age, BMI, AST/ALT ratio, platelet, albumin. AUROC 0.80. Dikombinasikan dengan FIB-4 untuk optimal accuracy.
DOI: 10.1002/hep.21496 🌟 1,850+ citations
LANDMARK: NAFLD & Metabolic Syndrome
Association of Non-Alcoholic Fatty Liver Disease with Metabolic Syndrome Independent of Central Obesity
Hirsch A, Schaffstein J, Zaichuk T, et al.
Scientific Reports • 2016 • N=2,147
Kontribusi: Meta-analysis: 40-50% NAFLD dengan metabolic syndrome. 50-70% NAFLD dengan Type 2 DM. Insulin resistance adalah core pathophysiology. Prevalence data untuk risk stratification.
DOI: 10.1038/srep28325 🌟 138 citations
REVIEW: Noninvasive Assessment in Type 2 DM
Noninvasive Assessment of Hepatic Fibrosis in Type 2 Diabetes: A Systematic Review
Garg H, Sarin SK, Kumar M, et al.
Acta Diabetologica • 2020 • Systematic Review (15 studies)
Kontribusi: Clinical utility comparison: FIB-4, NAFLD fibrosis score, transient elastography. Recommendations untuk T2DM+NAFLD screening. Evidence synthesis untuk algorithms.
DOI: 10.1007/s00592-019-01419-1 🌟 49 citations
RECENT: Transient Elastography (TE)
Transient Elastography for the Assessment of Liver Fibrosis and Steatosis in Patients with NAFLD
Petta S, Vanni E, Bugianesi E, et al.
Journal of Hepatology • 2018 • N=1,000+ (meta-analysis)
Kontribusi: Transient elastography (FibroScan) non-invasive imaging untuk NAFLD assessment. AUROC superior untuk fibrosis detection. Validated cutoffs. Combination dengan biochemical scores optimal.
DOI: 10.1016/j.jhep.2018.07.006 🌟 140 citations

✅ Rekomendasi Manajemen
  • Screening: Prioritas untuk pasien dengan metabolic syndrome, obesity, atau diabetes
  • Initial Assessment: Ultrasound screening + FIB-4 index calculation
  • Risk Stratification: Normal | Possible NASH | Advanced Fibrosis (berdasarkan FIB-4 cutoff)
  • Lifestyle Intervention: Weight loss 5-10%, exercise, diet modification
  • Referral to Hepatologist: If FIB-4 suggestive of advanced fibrosis atau ultrasound abnormal
  • Monitoring: Annual ultrasound + labs untuk high-risk patients
