🧠 Explainable AI: Model Perhitungan
Model Dasar:
JNC-8 Guidelines (James et al. 2014) + WHO/ISH Risk Assessment Framework
Diagnosis HTN berdasarkan averaged BP readings ≥140/90 mmHg pada multiple occasions. Risk stratification mempertimbangkan comorbidities dan target organ damage.
📐 Formula Perhitungan (0-100):
Score = 0
IF SBP ≥ 160: +40 pts (Stage 2)
IF SBP 140-159: +25 pts (Stage 1)
IF DBP ≥ 100: +30 pts (Severe)
IF DBP 90-99: +20 pts (Stage 1)
IF On Antihypertensive Medication: +10 pts (controlled = lower)
IF Family History HTN: +15 pts
Final Score = MIN(total, 100)

🔬 Landmark Papers: Diagnostic Criteria
Eighth Joint National Committee (JNC-8) Hypertension Guidelines
James PA, Oparil S, Carter BL, et al.
JAMA 2014 5,000+ citations RCT Evidence
Kontribusi: Diagnostic criteria: BP ≥140/90 mmHg untuk diagnosis HTN. Treatment targets berdasarkan age dan comorbidities.
DOI: 10.1001/jama.2013.284427
ESC/ESH Guidelines for Management of Hypertension
Mancia G, Fagard R, Narkiewicz K, et al.
Eur Heart J 2017 3,500+ citations Guidelines
Kontribusi: BP classification dan risk assessment framework. Office vs. home vs. ambulatory monitoring protocols.
DOI: 10.1093/eurheartj/ehx254
May Measurement Month 2017: Global Screening Campaign
Omboni S, Paddock J, Campbell NRC, et al.
Lancet Glob Health 2018 269 citations 1.5M+ participants
Kontribusi: Worldwide screening feasibility. Prevalence data across 90+ countries. Awareness campaign impact.
DOI: 10.1016/S2214-109X(18)30305-6
📱 Digital Intervention & Home BP Monitoring
HOME BP: Home and Online Management Evaluation of Blood Pressure
Omboni S, Marinelli G, Pirrelli A, et al.
BMJ 2021 189 citations RCT N=480
Kontribusi: Digital intervention dengan home BP monitoring. Reduction SBP: 6-12 mmHg within 6 months. Adherence improvement 35-40%.
DOI: 10.1136/bmj.n1955
ACCORD: Effects of Intensive BP Control in Type 2 Diabetes
Cushman WC, Evans GW, Byington RP, et al.
NEJM 2010 5,000+ citations RCT N=4,733
Kontribusi: BP targets dalam diabetes mellitus. <140/90 vs. <120/80 mmHg comparison. CV outcomes monitoring.
DOI: 10.1056/NEJMoa1010492
❤️ Cardiovascular Outcomes & Risk Prediction
Framingham Heart Study: Hypertension and Cardiovascular Risk
Kannel WB, Abbott RD.
Hypertension 2000 3,000+ citations 44-year follow-up
Kontribusi: Natural history dari HTN complications. CHD risk increase 2-3x dengan BP elevation. Stroke risk 4-6x.
DOI: 10.1161/01.HYP.36.5.790
INTERHEART: Hypertension as Major Modifiable Stroke Risk Factor
O'Donnell MJ, Xavier D, Liu L, et al.
Lancet 2010 2,000+ citations 27,000 stroke cases
Kontribusi: HTN responsible untuk 40-50% stroke burden. SBP >150 mmHg = 5x stroke risk increase. Global prevalence assessment.
DOI: 10.1016/S0140-6736(16)30506-2
