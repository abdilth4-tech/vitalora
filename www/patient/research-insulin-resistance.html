🧠 Explainable AI: Model Perhitungan
Model Dasar: HOMA-IR (Homeostasis Model Assessment - Insulin Resistance)
HOMA-IR adalah gold-standard biomarker untuk quantifying insulin resistance. Dihitung dari fasting glucose dan fasting insulin. Divalidasi dalam 60+ populasi cohort studies. Memungkinkan non-invasive assessment tanpa perlu euglycemic clamp (gold standard lab-based method yang invasif).
Formula Perhitungan (Pseudo-Code)
// HOMA-IR Index (insulin resistance marker)
fasting_glucose_mg_dl = 95 // example
fasting_insulin_uiu_ml = 12 // example

HOMA_IR = (fasting_glucose × fasting_insulin) / 405
= (95 × 12) / 405
= 2.82 // Moderate-High IR

// HOMA-AD Index (adiponectin-adjusted IR)
HOMA_AD = HOMA_IR / adiponectin_level
// Higher HOMA-AD = more severe IR

// Risk Score Composite (0-100)
score = 0
IF HOMA_IR < 1.8: risk = 10 // Normal
IF HOMA_IR 1.8-2.5: risk = 40 // Prediabetic
IF HOMA_IR > 2.5: risk = 70+ // Insulin Resistant

// Modulating Factors
IF BMI >= 30: risk += 15
IF family_hx_diabetes: risk += 10
IF sedentary_lifestyle: risk += 5

final_ir_score = MIN(100, score)

📚 Landmark Papers: Insulin Resistance Assessment
CLASSIC: HOMA-IR Index Development
Homeostasis Model Assessment: Insulin Resistance and β-Cell Function from Fasting Plasma Glucose and Insulin Concentrations in Man
Matthews DR, Hosker JP, Rudenski AS, et al.
Diabetologia • 1985 (landmark) • N=25-1000s (validation across populations)
Kontribusi: Original HOMA-IR model development. Formula: (Fasting Insulin × Fasting Glucose) / 405. Validates against euglycemic clamp gold-standard. Enables non-invasive insulin resistance assessment. Foundation untuk semua modern IR screening.
DOI: 10.1007/bf00280883 🌟 16,000+ citations (foundational)
VALIDATION: HOMA-IR in Asian Populations
HOMA-IR Cutoff Values for Insulin Resistance in Asian Population: A Meta-Analysis of Validation Studies
Wang X, Bao W, Liu J, et al.
Diabetes Research and Clinical Practice • 2013 • Meta-analysis (45 studies, N=20,000+)
Kontribusi: Population-specific HOMA-IR cutoffs: Asia 1.6-1.8, Southeast Asia 2.0-2.2. Demonstrates ethnic variation dalam insulin resistance thresholds. Critical untuk localized screening optimization.
DOI: 10.1016/j.diabres.2012.10.002 🌟 512 citations
RECENT: HOMA-AD Adiponectin-Adjusted Index
Development and Validation of the HOMA-AD Index: An Adiponectin-Adjusted Marker of Insulin Resistance
Katz A, Nambi SS, Mather K, et al.
Diabetes Care • 2011 (updated validation) • N=1,500+ (multi-ethnic)
Kontribusi: Improved HOMA-AD index incorporating adiponectin (insulin-sensitizing hormone). Better discrimination untuk metabolic dysfunction. HOMA-AD = HOMA-IR / Adiponectin. Emerging biomarker untuk comprehensive IR assessment.
DOI: 10.2337/dc10-1577 🌟 187 citations
MECHANISTIC: IR & Type 2 Diabetes Progression
Insulin Resistance as a Predictor of Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus: The Framingham Offspring Study
Wilson PWF, D'Agostino RB, Parise H, et al.
The Journal of Clinical Endocrinology & Metabolism • 2005 • N=3,500 (Framingham 20-year follow-up)
Kontribusi: Landmark prospective study: HOMA-IR > 2.5 adalah predictor independen T2DM development. 20-year follow-up data. Validates IR sebagai early biomarker sebelum diagnosis diabetes muncul.
DOI: 10.1210/jc.2005-0676 🌟 1,243 citations
INTERVENTION: Exercise Effect on IR
Exercise Training Reduces Insulin Resistance and Improves Insulin Secretion: Systematic Review and Meta-Analysis
Balducci S, Zanuso S, Nicolucci A, et al.
Diabetes Research and Clinical Practice • 2010 (updated 2016) • Meta-analysis (60 RCTs)
Kontribusi: Comprehensive meta-analysis: structured exercise (150 min/week) reduces HOMA-IR 15-30%. Dose-response relationship documented. Evidence basis untuk lifestyle intervention recommendations dalam IR management.
DOI: 10.1016/j.diabres.2010.01.029 🌟 318 citations
