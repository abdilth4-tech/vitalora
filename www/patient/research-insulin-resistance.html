🧪 Spesifikasi Tool Screening (VITALORA)
Insulin Resistance Assessment Multi-Step
  • Step 1: Anthropometry (weight, height, waist circumference)
  • Step 2: Symptoms questionnaire (fatigue, hyperphagia, skin manifestations)
  • Step 3: Risk factors (obesity, sedentary, family history DM)
  • Step 4: Diet assessment (high glycemic load, refined carbs)
  • Step 5: Lab work recommendation (fasting glucose, insulin, adiponectin)
  • Step 6: HOMA-IR calculation & interpretation
✓ Validasi: HOMA-IR dari 10+ validation studies, well-established biomarker
✓ Scoring: 0-100 scale with population-specific HOMA-IR cutoff integration

✅ Rekomendasi Manajemen (Evidence-Based)
  • Screening target: Obese, sedentary, family history DM, MetS patients
  • Diagnosis: HOMA-IR > 2.5 (global) atau population-specific cutoff
  • Assessment: Kombinasikan HOMA-IR + HOMA-AD untuk comprehensive evaluation
  • Lifestyle intervention: Weight loss 5-10%, exercise 150 min/week → significant IR improvement
  • Monitor progression: Annual HOMA-IR re-assessment untuk high-risk patients
