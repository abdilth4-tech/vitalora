✅ Rekomendasi Manajemen (Evidence-Based)
  • Screening target: High-risk populations (obese, hypertensive, age > 50, male gender)
  • Diagnosis: STOP-BANG score ≥ 3 indicates moderate-high risk; refer for sleep study
  • Portable vs lab: Portable devices (SpO2 + airflow) for home screening; polysomnography as gold standard
  • Management: Mild-moderate: lifestyle (weight loss, sleep position, alcohol avoidance); Severe: CPAP therapy
  • Follow-up: Re-screen annually if high-risk; monitor compliance with treatment
