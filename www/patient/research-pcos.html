🧪 Spesifikasi Tool Screening (VITALORA)
Kuesioner PCOS Multi-Step (Rotterdam 2018)
  • Step 1: Demografi & riwayat menstruasi (regularity, dysmenorrhea)
  • Step 2: Gejala hyperandrogenism (acne, hirsutism, hair loss)
  • Step 3: Riwayat subfertility atau infertility treatment
  • Step 4: Metabolic factors (weight, waist circumference, skin condition)
  • Step 5: Riwayat medis & keluarga (DM, HTN, dyslipidemia)
  • Step 6: Lab work recommendation & referral assessment

✅ Rekomendasi Manajemen (Evidence-Based)
  • Screening: Utama untuk women of reproductive age dengan menstrual irregularity atau infertility
  • Diagnosis: Rotterdam 2018 criteria (≥2 dari 3: anovulation, hyperandrogenism, polycystic ovaries)
  • Metabolic assessment: Screen untuk MetS, DM, dyslipidemia di semua PCOS patients
  • Lifestyle intervention: Weight loss 5-10% → improved ovulation & metabolic profile (25-30% remission)
  • Personalized approach: Management disesuaikan dengan metabolic phenotype (classic vs ovulatory vs non-hyperandrogenic)
