🌿 Traditional Herbal Formulas

Evidence-informed traditional Indonesian herbal formulas (Jamu) for metabolic disease prevention and management. Each formula combines multiple herbals with synergistic properties, organized by indication. Preparation methods and dosage based on traditional protocols and modern clinical experience.

💊 Anti-Lipid Formulas (Dyslipidemia)
Formula 1: Turmeric-Garlic Lipid Tonic
Traditional name: Jamu Kunyit Bawang (Curcuma-Allium combination)
📋 Ingredients
Turmeric root (Curcuma longa) 15 g standardized 3% curcuminoid ideal
Garlic bulb (Allium sativum) — aged 10 g fermented preferred (10-20% allicin)
Ginger rhizome (Zingiber officinale) 8 g dried preferred for concentration
Olive leaves (Olea europaea) 5 g dried or extract form
Licorice root (Glycyrrhiza glabra) 3 g sweetening + inflammatory support
🔥 Preparation
1
Chop all ingredients finely. Bring 800 mL water to boil in ceramic/glass pot (avoid metal reactivity with turmeric).
2
Add all ingredients. Reduce heat to low simmer for 30-40 minutes until liquid reduces to ~500 mL (1:1.6 concentration ratio).
3
Strain through fine mesh filter. Cool to room temperature. Add 1 tsp raw honey (post-cooling, preserves enzymes).
4
Store in glass bottle in cool, dark place. Shelf life: 5-7 days refrigerated (or freeze in ice cube trays for 3 months).
💊 Dosage & Indication
Dose: 150 mL (5 oz) daily, preferably in morning on empty stomach or with light breakfast
Duration: Minimum 8-12 weeks for measurable lipid improvement
Indication: Dyslipidemia Atherosclerosis Risk
Expected Benefit: LDL ↓15-25%, TG ↓15-20% based on herbal GRADE evidence
🩹 Anti-Hypertension Formulas
Formula 2: Garlic-Olive Leaf Blood Pressure Support
Traditional name: Jamu Bawang Putih Daun Zaitun (Garlic-Olive blend)
📋 Ingredients
Aged garlic extract powder 6 g or 15g fresh fermented garlic
Olive leaf extract 4 g standardized polyphenol content ideal
Hibiscus flowers (Hibiscus sabdariffa) 5 g additional BP support + flavor
Celery seeds (Apium graveolens) 3 g traditional HTN remedy
Hawthorn leaves/berries (Crataegus spp.) 4 g cardiovascular support
🔥 Preparation
1
Boil 700 mL water. Add all herbs. Simmer 25-30 minutes.
2
Strain. Divide into 2 portions (350 mL each) for morning and evening dosing.
💊 Dosage & Indication
Dose: 350 mL twice daily (morning & evening)
Duration: 12 weeks for stable BP reduction
Indication: Hypertension Cardiovascular Risk
Expected Benefit: SBP ↓5-8 mmHg, DBP ↓2-4 mmHg
🍬 Anti-Diabetes Formulas

Traditional name: Jamu Kayu Manis Jahe Kunyit (3-spice metabolic blend)

Ginger rhizome (Zingiber officinale) 6 g fresh preferred
Turmeric root (Curcuma longa) 8 g dried preferred
Fenugreek seeds (Trigonella foenum-graecum) 4 g glucose modulation
Bitter melon leaves (Momordica charantia) 5 g traditional glucose support
💊 Dosage & Indication
Dose: 200 mL once daily (morning, before breakfast)
Duration: 12+ weeks for HbA1c improvement
Indication: Type 2 Diabetes Prediabetes Metabolic Syndrome
Expected Benefit: FPG ↓10-15%, HbA1c ↓0.5-0.8%
⚠️ Note: Ceylon cinnamon ONLY to avoid coumarin interaction if on anticoagulants

⚖️ Metabolic Balance Formula (Comprehensive MetS)
Formula 5: The Five-Herb Metabolic Harmony Blend
Traditional name: Jamu Harmoni Metabolik (Comprehensive multi-herbal for all 5 MetS components)
📋 Ingredients
Turmeric root (Curcuma longa) 12 g central anti-inflammatory agent
Green tea leaves (Camellia sinensis) 6 g EGCG + antioxidant
Ginger rhizome (Zingiber officinale) 7 g glucose + lipid support
Garlic bulb (Allium sativum) — aged 8 g BP + lipid support
Cinnamon bark (Cinnamomum verum) - CEYLON ONLY 3 g glucose modulation
🔥 Preparation (Cold Brew Option)
1
Cold Brew Method (24 hours): Place all ingredients in 1 L room-temperature water. Cover. Steep 24 hours at room temp.
2
Strain through fine mesh. Divide into 5 × 200 mL portions. Drink one portion each morning for 5 consecutive days per week.
3
Or Hot Brew: Simmer 20 minutes in 1 L water. Strain. Refrigerate for 5 days maximum.
💊 Dosage & Indication
Dose: 200 mL daily, 5 days/week (rest 2 days for tolerance)
Duration: 12 weeks minimum for multi-component MetS improvement
Indication: Metabolic Syndrome Multiple HTN+Lipid+Glucose+Obesity
Expected Benefit: Overall MetS components ↓ 15-25%, CRP ↓ 20-30%
⚠️ Important: Ceylon cinnamon ONLY. Consult doctor if on anticoagulants.
🛍️ Sourcing & Storage Guidelines
Quality Selection:
• Purchase whole dried herbs when possible (better preservation of active compounds)
• Check for pesticide-free certification (organic preferred)
• Store in airtight glass containers, away from light & heat
• Discard if mold, off-odor, or pest presence noted

Local Sourcing (Indonesia):
• Traditional herbal markets (pasar jamu) in major cities
• Certified organic suppliers (verify certification)
• Licensed health food stores

Online Sourcing:
• Verify supplier credentials & third-party testing (heavy metals, microbial contamination)
• Check for GMP (Good Manufacturing Practice) certification

Shelf Life:
• Dried herbs: 6-12 months in cool, dark place
• Fresh decoctions: 5-7 days refrigerated
• Frozen portions: Up to 3 months in freezer
