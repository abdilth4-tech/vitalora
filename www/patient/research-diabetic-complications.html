🧪 Spesifikasi Tool Screening (VITALORA)
Diabetic Complications Multi-Step Screening
  • Step 1: HbA1c & diabetes duration assessment
  • Step 2: Retinopathy screening (vision changes, blurred vision)
  • Step 3: Nephropathy screening (edema, urine changes)
  • Step 4: Neuropathy screening (foot numbness, pain, sensations)
  • Step 5: Cardiovascular risk (chest pain, palpitations, shortness of breath)
  • Step 6: Referral recommendations (ophthalmology, nephrology, cardiology)

✅ Rekomendasi Manajemen (Evidence-Based)
  • Screening frequency: Annual untuk T1D + T2DM; baseline + annual thereafter
  • Diagnosis: Multimodal assessment (retinoscopy, OCT, urine microalbumin, foot exam, EKG)
  • Risk stratification: HbA1c, duration, family history, HTN status
  • Prevention: Tight HbA1c control, BP management, lipid control → 25-75% risk reduction
  • Referral: Abnormal findings → specialist referral (ophthalmology, endocrinology, cardiology)
