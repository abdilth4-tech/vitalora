✅ Rekomendasi Manajemen (Evidence-Based)
  • Screening target: Adults with hyperuricemia history, recurrent joint pain, family history
  • Diagnosis: Serum uric acid > 6.8 mg/dL (male) or > 6.0 (female) + clinical symptoms
  • Lifestyle first: Low-purine diet, limit alcohol, weight management, hydration
  • Pharmacotherapy: Allopurinol if recurrent attacks (target UA < 6 mg/dL)
  • Monitor comorbidities: Screen untuk HTN, DM, CKD yang meningkatkan risk
