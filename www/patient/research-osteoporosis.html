🧪 Spesifikasi Tool Screening (VITALORA)
Osteoporosis Risk Assessment Multi-Step
  • Step 1: Demographics (age, gender, ethnicity, menopause status)
  • Step 2: Anthropometry (weight, height, BMI calculation)
  • Step 3: Risk factors (family history, previous fractures, steroid use)
  • Step 4: Lifestyle (calcium/vitamin D intake, physical activity, smoking)
  • Step 5: Medical history (diabetes, rheumatoid arthritis, CKD)
  • Step 6: FRAX calculation & DXA referral
✓ Validasi: FRAX tool dari 12+ validation studies
✓ Scoring: 0-100 scale with FRAX 10-year fracture risk integration

✅ Rekomendasi Manajemen (Evidence-Based)
  • Screening target: Postmenopausal women ≥50 years, men ≥70 years, or with risk factors
  • Tool: FRAX calculator (non-invasive, integrates multiple risk factors)
  • Diagnosis: DXA if FRAX indicates increased 10-year fracture risk
  • Prevention: Calcium 1000-1200 mg/day + Vitamin D 800-1000 IU/day
  • Pharmacotherapy: Bisphosphonates jika high fracture risk (FRAX > thresholds)
