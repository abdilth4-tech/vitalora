NAFLD/MASLD Screening
📊 Tinjauan Topik

Non-Alcoholic Fatty Liver Disease (NAFLD/MASLD) adalah gangguan metabolik hepatik paling umum. Sumber: 10 paper peer-reviewed (2025-2026) tentang noninvasive screening, fibrosis assessment, dan metabolomics markers.

MASLD, Obesity & Youth-Onset T2DM Pathway
Parizad, R. | World J Clin Pediatrics
 2026
MASLD mempengaruhi ~13% anak global, 47% dengan obesity; risiko T2D meningkat 2.7x; ceramide & pro-inflammatory cytokine mekanisme; GLP-1RA menjanjikan
Validasi biomarker noninvasive dalam pediatri; akses equitable targeted therapy; disparitas sosioekonomi
 DOI
2
Simple Screening Strategy for MASLD
Katahira, M. | Metabolic Syndrome & Related Disorders
 2026
Sistem scoring 6-variabel (age, BMI, HDL-C, HbA1c, ALT, albumin) mencapai AUC lebih tinggi daripada FLI, HSI, NAFLD ridge; berguna untuk health screening centers
Validasi prospektif; implementasi setting primary care; cost-effectiveness analysis
 DOI
3
Lipid-Related IR Indices for NAFLD Diagnosis
Cao, W.H. | Biomedical & Environmental Sciences
 2026
TyG-BMI optimal dalam NAFLD dengan abnormalitas metabolik (AUC 0.819, cutoff 39.77); indeks TyG lebih baik untuk NAFLD tanpa MA (AUC 0.724, cutoff 1.580)
Validasi dalam kelompok etnis diverse; standardisasi cutoff values; prediksi prospektif DKD/NASH risk
 DOI
4
Life's Simple 7 & MASLD Association
Hofer, H. | Open Heart
 2026
LS7 score inverse terkait MASLD (RR 0.80); 82% MASLD poor LS7, 47% intermediate, 16% ideal; asosiasi weak fibrosis
Perubahan longitudinal LS7 dan hepatic outcome; integrasi dengan noninvasive fibrosis markers; trial intervensi
 DOI
5
ML-Driven Hepatic Fibrosis Staging in NASH
Faisal, S. | Computers in Biology & Medicine
 2026
Model RF & XGBoost dengan microbiome mencapai 99.1% balanced accuracy; advanced fibrosis (F3/F4) menunjukkan dysbiosis pathogenik; superior daripada APRI, FIB-4
Validasi eksternal dalam kohort independen; feasibility cost; generalisasi ke populasi non-biopsy

💡 Ringkasan untuk Vitalora
  • Screening terdepan: TyG-BMI untuk MASLD dengan abnormalitas metabolik; 6-variable score untuk health screening
  • Fibrosis assessment: Transient elastography + FIB-4 untuk noninvasive staging
  • Early detection: Metabolomics markers (1-methyluric acid, maresin 1) untuk diagnosis accuracy
  • Comorbidity awareness: Periksa T2DM, dyslipidemia, obesity concurrent
  • Intervention focus: Lifestyle modifications; GLP-1RA untuk weight loss; monitor hepatic function
