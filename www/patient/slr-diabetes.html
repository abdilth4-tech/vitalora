📊 Tinjauan Topik

Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus adalah penyakit metabolik kronis dengan prevalensi tinggi. Sumber: 10 paper peer-reviewed (2025-2026) tentang screening, biomarker novel, dan stratifikasi risiko untuk deteksi dini.

Pioglitazone vs GLP-1RA in T2DM Management

AlbF elevated independen terkait DR (adjusted OR 1.88 per 10 μmol/g); hubungan dose-response evident; biomarker screening potensial

9

Rasio TG/HDL-C prediktor sex-spesifik (pria OR 1.28, wanita OR 1.35); optimal cutoff berbeda sex (3.72 vs 2.73); biomarker sederhana akses
Implementasi klinis cutoff sex-stratified; validasi prospektif dalam populasi diverse

Kausalitas MGO dalam microvascular disease; RCT intervensi menargetkan AGE formation pada diabetes

💡 Ringkasan & Rekomendasi untuk Vitalora
  • Biomarker screening early detection: TyG, TyG-BMI, TG/HDL-C (sex-specific), osteocalcin
  • DKD prevention focus: Monitor insulin resistance markers; screen microalbuminuria
  • Comorbidity awareness: Check MASLD risk; assess cardiovascular subclinical damage
  • Pharmacotherapy optimization: GLP-1RA preferred; metformin first-line; consider osteogenic markers
  • Population-specific targets: Sex-stratified cutoffs; ethnic-specific validation needed
