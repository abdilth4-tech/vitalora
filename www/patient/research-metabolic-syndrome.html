IDF Diagnostic Criteria (5 components, 3+ = MetS)
1️⃣ Central Obesity (WC) M ≥94, F ≥80 (cm)
2️⃣ Triglycerides ≥150 mg/dL
3️⃣ HDL Cholesterol M <40, F <50 (mg/dL)
4️⃣ Blood Pressure ≥130/85 mmHg
5️⃣ Fasting Glucose ≥100 mg/dL

🧪 Spesifikasi Tool Screening
MetS Screening Questionnaire
  • Step 1: Central obesity assessment (waist circumference)
  • Step 2: Metabolic symptoms (fatigue, increased thirst/urination)
  • Step 3: Risk factors (family history, sedentary lifestyle, diet)
  • Step 4: Lab values (triglycerides, HDL, glucose, BP)
  • Step 5: Component count & risk stratification
  • Step 6: IDF criteria assessment & management plan
✓ Based on IDF diagnostic framework
✓ Integrated with PREDIMED-Plus lifestyle intervention protocol
✓ Output: Component count, severity score, referral recommendation

✅ Rekomendasi Manajemen
  • Screening: Semua dewasa usia 20+ dengan ≥2 risk factors
  • Diagnosis: IDF criteria (central obesity + 2 more components)
  • Lifestyle Priority: Weight loss 5-10%, Mediterranean diet, 150 min/week exercise
  • Goal Achievement: 40-50% achieve MetS remission in 6-12 months with intervention
  • Follow-up: Monitor every 3 months during intervention phase
  • Referral: If components not improving or metabolic complications develop
