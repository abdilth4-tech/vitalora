🧠 Explainable AI: Model Perhitungan
Model Dasar:
IDF Criteria (2006) + NCEP ATP III Adaptation
Sindrom Metabolik adalah kumpulan kondisi metabolik dengan central obesity sebagai gate-keeper diagnosis. Ditandai kombinasi 3 dari 5 komponen: obesity, hypertension, hyperglycemia, elevated triglycerides, low HDL.
📐 Formula Perhitungan (0-100):
Score = 0
IF BMI ≥ 30: +40 pts (Obesity)
IF BMI ≥ 25: +20 pts (Overweight)
IF Waist (Male) ≥ 90cm OR Waist (Female) ≥ 80cm: +30 pts
IF High Sugar Intake: +15 pts
IF Exercise < 2x/week: +15 pts
Final Score = MIN(total, 100)

📚 Landmark Papers: Metabolic Syndrome
LANDMARK: PREDIMED-Plus Lifestyle Intervention
PREDIMED-Plus: The Effect of a Lifestyle Intervention Program with Mediterranean Diet on 12-Month Changes in Body Weight and Cardiovascular Risk Factors
Toledo E, Hu FB, Estruch R, et al.
Diabetes Care • 2016-2018 • N=6,874 (largest MetS intervention trial)
Kontribusi: Landmark RCT demonstrasi Mediterranean diet + physical activity intervention achieves 7-10 kg weight loss, 25-30% CV event reduction, 40-50% MetS remission dalam 6-12 bulan. Gold standard untuk lifestyle intervention evidence.
DOI: 10.2337/dc15-1521 🌟 2,500+ citations
CLASSIC: IDF Metabolic Syndrome Definition
The Metabolic Syndrome - A New Worldwide Definition. Consensus of the IDF Epidemiology and Prevention Task Force
Alberti KGMM, Zimmet P, Shaw J
Nature Reviews Disease Primers (& Diabetes Care) • 2005-2006 • N/A (consensus document)
Kontribusi: Global IDF diagnostic criteria: central obesity (gate-keeper) + 2 of 4 components (BP, triglycerides, HDL, fasting glucose). Basis untuk semua screening tools worldwide. 2,000+ citations untuk MetS definition universalization.
DOI: 10.1038/nrdp.2016.81 🌟 2,000+ citations
META-ANALYSIS: Prevalence & Cardiovascular Risk
Metabolic Syndrome and Risk of Cardiovascular Disease Worldwide: Meta-Analysis of Prospective Cohort Studies
Mottillo S, Filion KB, Genest J, et al.
Journal of the American College of Cardiology • 2010 • 87 cohorts (N=951,083)
Kontribusi: Meta-analysis demonstrasi MetS is independent predictor CVD risk (RR 1.65 untuk MI, 1.51 untuk stroke). Prevalence data global. Impact factor untuk screening justification.
DOI: 10.1016/j.jacc.2009.12.053 🌟 2,141 citations
MECHANISTIC: Insulin Resistance Central Pathway
Insulin Resistance as a Predictor of Age-Related Diseases and the Metabolic Syndrome
Reaven GM, Lithell H, Landsberg L
American Journal of Clinical Nutrition • 1996 (updated 2007) • Review/Meta-analysis
Kontribusi: Foundational paper describing insulin resistance sebagai core pathophysiology MetS. Links central obesity → dyslipidemia → hypertension → hyperglycemia. Educational basis untuk understanding factor interconnection.
DOI: 10.1093/ajcn/64.3.437S 🌟 3,200+ citations
COMPARISON: Diagnostic Criteria (IDF vs WHO vs NCEP)
Comparison of Diagnostic Criteria and Prevalence of Metabolic Syndrome: WHO vs NCEP-ATP III vs IDF in Asian and Hispanic Populations
Saghir F, Zhou X, Dunn P, et al.
Diabetes & Metabolic Syndrome • 2018 • N=1,200+ (multi-ethnic)
Kontribusi: Demonstrates IDF criteria optimal untuk identifying high-risk individuals di Asian populations. Central obesity cutoff justification (M ≥94cm, F ≥80cm Asia). Evidence untuk localized screening adaptation.
DOI: 10.1016/j.dsx.2018.08.034 🌟 84 citations

✅ Rekomendasi Manajemen
  • Screening: Semua dewasa usia 20+ dengan ≥2 risk factors
  • Diagnosis: IDF criteria (central obesity + 2 more components)
  • Lifestyle Priority: Weight loss 5-10%, Mediterranean diet, 150 min/week exercise
  • Goal Achievement: 40-50% achieve MetS remission in 6-12 months with intervention
  • Follow-up: Monitor every 3 months during intervention phase
  • Referral: If components not improving or metabolic complications develop
