🧪 Spesifikasi Tool Screening (VITALORA)
CKD Comprehensive Screening Multi-Step
  • Step 1: Risk factors (age, diabetes, hypertension, family history)
  • Step 2: Symptoms (fatigue, edema, nocturia, appetite changes)
  • Step 3: Lab work: Serum creatinine (calculate eGFR), albumin-to-creatinine ratio
  • Step 4: eGFR staging (KDIGO 1-5, GFR ≥90 to <15)
  • Step 5: Albuminuria classification (normal, microalbuminuria, macroalbuminuria)
  • Step 6: CVD risk assessment + referral recommendations

✓ Scoring: 0-100 scale with CKD stage + albuminuria risk integration

✅ Rekomendasi Manajemen (Evidence-Based)
  • Screening: All patients with DM, HTN, age >60; annually if risk factors
  • Diagnosis: eGFR (from creatinine) + ACR (from spot urine or 24h urine)
  • Staging: KDIGO 1-5 based on eGFR; albuminuria classification (normal/micro/macro)
  • Prevention: BP control (<120/90 for DM), ACE-I/ARB, SGLT2 inhibitors for DM
  • Referral: Nephrologist if eGFR <30 or rapid decline (>30% in 1 year)
