✅ Rekomendasi Manajemen
  • Screening: Semua dewasa, special attention jika BMI ≥23 (Asia) or ≥25 (global)
  • Assessment: BMI + waist circumference + comorbidity screening
  • Diagnosis: Gunakan Asian-specific cutoffs: Normal <23, Obese Grade 1 23-24.9, Grade 2 ≥25
  • Intervention: Structured lifestyle program (diet + exercise + behavioral counseling)
  • Weight Loss Goals: 5-15% body weight reduction over 6-12 months
  • Maintenance: Continuous monitoring, ongoing activity tracking, long-term support
  • Referral: Jika BMI ≥30 (Grade 2) dengan comorbidities → bariatric surgery evaluation
