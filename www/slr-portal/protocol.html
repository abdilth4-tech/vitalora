SLR Protocol
Overview

PRISMA-2020 compliant systematic literature review protocol for screening 15 metabolic diseases. 1,020+ papers identified, 15 landmark papers included (Grade A-B).

Research Questions
Primary Questions:
  • Pathophysiological pathways and risk factor cascades?
  • Most sensitive/specific biomarkers for screening?
  • Evidence-based risk stratification algorithms?
  • Landmark epidemiological studies & prevalence?
  • Evidence-based clinical cutoff points?
Inclusion Criteria
Study Design: RCTs, cohorts, case-control, systematic reviews, meta-analyses
Population: Adults ≥18 years with or at risk for 15 target diseases
Outcomes: Biomarker definition, risk prediction, diagnostic criteria, prevalence, incidence
Publication: Peer-reviewed journal articles & published guidelines (WHO, ADA, ESC)
Time: 1995-2026 (emphasis on landmark trials & recent reviews)
Target Diseases (15)
1. Dyslipidemia
2. Hypertension
3. NAFLD
4. Metabolic Syndrome
5. Obesity & IR
6. Type 2 Diabetes
7. CKD
8. Depression
9. CAD Risk
10. Stroke Risk
11. Atrial Fibrillation
12. Sleep Apnea
13. Thyroid Disorders
14. PCOS
15. Gout
Search Strategy
Databases: PubMed, Cochrane, Web of Science, Scopus, Clinical Guidelines
Filters: 1995-2026, English, Journal articles & guidelines
Example Search:
(disease_name) AND (screening OR diagnosis OR risk assessment) AND (sensitivity OR specificity OR AUROC OR validation)
